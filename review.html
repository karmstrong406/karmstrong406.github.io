Kelly's review of web hosters

This is a very brief review and comparison of webhost servers bluehost, godaddy, and hostgator. It lists out their benefits in an easy to read design.

Host #1 Bluehost

Here are the benefits:

  • only $3.95 a month
  • free domain name for the first year
  • money back guarantee
  • 24/7 support
  • SSL Certificate

Host #2 GoDaddy

Here are the benefits:

  • come up with your own domain name
  • free trial
  • provides discount for Office 365, WordPress and other items
  • SSL Certificate
  • can pay extra for a professional team to build an amazing website
  • 24/7 Customer Service
  • "simple, fast, and easy!"

Host #3 HostGator

Here are the benefits:

  • starting price at $2.75 a month
  • free SSL Certificate
  • free domain for a year
  • can add on supports like WordPress for additional costs
  • 24/7 support
  • 45 Day money back guarantee
  • site is available and running 99.9% of the time

This concludes my brief review of these three webhost servers. I cannot conlude which of these three servers provides the best servers because I don't know the added value of having add ons such as WordPress and easy to use website builders.
